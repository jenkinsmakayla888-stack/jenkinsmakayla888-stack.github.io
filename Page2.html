Ethan Becker


About

Ethan Becker is an American visual development artist, storyteller, and animator originally from southeast Texas. He was inspired early on by Studio Ghibli films, nature, and fantasy worlds, which led him to pursue art as a profession. Becker attended high school art programs, won a scholarship to a community college, and later studied at the Concept Design Academy in Pasadena

Professionally, Ethan has worked on shows like Voltron: Legendary Defender and Batman: The Long Halloween, and with studios such as DreamWorks TV, Netflix, and Warner Bros. He’s also known for his teaching and content creation—he shares his process, critiques, and art tips heavily through YouTube and other online platforms, helping aspiring artists improve in design, environment art, and storytelling.

Primary Mediums

  • Digital Painting
  • 2D animation software
  • Storyboarding tools
  • Sketching and drawing
  • Video editing and motion graphics
