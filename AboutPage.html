About Me

Makayla Jenkins

I'm a IT major with minors in cybersecurity and Spanish here at KSU

Background

This project was my love letter to art. Real art. I wanted to highlight some of my biggest inspirations as a creative, and in doing so hope that I am introducing others to these talanted people, with different styles and mediums. Anyone can do art. It's the message I hope you take away from this website. With the rise of AI generated images (I refuse to call them art), art has lost its soul and is slowly becoming only a comodity. Not a skill, or a process, but something to be quickly and cheaply mass produced and absorbed. Like fast food.

My Interests

As an artist myself, I have gravitated to character design and portraits, as obvious as it is with my selection of artists to highlight. The capturing of people is something I feel is a bit sacred. To be able to create something that captures a bit of the esssence of someone is very powerful. I love traditional and digital mediums of art, including oil pastels, acylic paints, watercolor paints, and just regular pencil and paper.
